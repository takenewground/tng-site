Unlock the power of savvy - illuminate your path to influence, meaning, and legacy!

To all the trailblazers, mavens of knowledge, and champions of integrity, get ready to unleash your full power at ICON. We have meticulously crafted this 3 day event with your unique brilliance in mind.

What to expect

Day 1: Understanding and Resolving Limitations

The first day focuses on identifying critical factors that hinder your development, uncovering blindspots, and working with challenges to prioritize what truly matters to you and build momentum with others.

Day 2: Recognizing and Expanding Legacy

The second day involves accurately assessing your current position, reviewing your journey, evaluating your present situation, and determining the desired future state of your legacy. It also explores strategies for resilience, adaptability, conflict engagement, and fostering meaningful connections.

Day 3: Crafting Vision and Maximizing Impact

On the last day we delve into understanding the different stages of life and their implications, visionary thinking to design a powerful future vision, overcoming challenges by refining and aligning your vision, and expanding your influence through playful celebrations of life and networking to reshape possibilities.

Michelin Star Cuisine

The final evening's meal will be prepared by the Michelin Star chef at the award winning restaurant Matteo Grand in Basilica, situated in front of the world famous architecture of the Villas Palladiana.

Location, Location, Location

Lefay Resort & SPA Lago di Garda is located in Gargnano, in the heart of the spectacular “Riviera dei Limoni.” A location for anyone who still wishes to be surprised and who loves to get lost and then find themselves again.
